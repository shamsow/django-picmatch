PicMatch

Supply a source image and a reference and this app will histogram match the source to the reference!

I built this app mainly to play with the histogram matching feature in scikit-image.

The app itself is quite simple in nature and the matching is limited in terms of accuracy.

Resulting images are often washed out and weird looking but it's still a cool concept.

The supplied source and reference should be similar in overall color, otherwise the results look really odd.

I've also found that larger quality images really put a strain on my laptop. I don't know if this due to my laptop not being up to the task or if scikit-image isn't great at handling high-res images. I'm guessing it's the former.

Lastly, the supplied images should be "clean", and by that I mean without artifacts. If the result image has artifacts, it's not the app that did it, the source image had artifacts to begin with which were brought out by the change in color.

Start matching!
